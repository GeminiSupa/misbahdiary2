# Backlink Outreach Plan (Pakistan Legal Niche)

## Priority link targets (highest leverage)

### 1) Bar councils / bar associations (.org.pk)
- Pakistan Bar Council (PBC)
- Punjab Bar Council
- Sindh Bar Council
- Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Bar Council
- Balochistan Bar Council
- District bar associations (Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, etc.)

**Pitch angle**: free resource/tools page addition for advocates (calendar, matter diary, billing templates, client updates).

### 2) Universities / law departments (.edu.pk)
- University law faculties
- Career offices
- Student law clinics
- Moot court societies
- Legal aid centers

**Pitch angle**: practice management resource for graduates + internship clinics; “digital lawyer diary” learning resource.

### 3) Legal journals / institutes
- University journals
- Research institutes
- Continuing legal education pages

**Pitch angle**: cite the guide as a practical reference + link to city page relevant to the institute.

## Outreach checklist (simple, repeatable)
- Collect contact emails (department office, web admin, registrar office, bar secretary)
- Track outreach in a sheet (sent date, follow-up date, response, link placed)
- Follow up once after 5–7 days
- Keep outreach personalized (city + institution name + why relevant)
